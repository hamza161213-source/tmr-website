About Us

The Method Room is a boutique, women-only Reformer Pilates studio in South London. We offer small-group classes designed to build strength, improve posture and help you move with confidence. Whether you're new to Pilates or progressing your practice, our welcoming space is built around you.

From your first visit, you'll feel looked after. We keep our studio intentionally intimate and supportive, with a maximum of eight reformers per class. This means genuine personal attention from your instructor, modifications tailored to your body, and the space to focus on your movement without distraction. You'll leave each session feeling stronger, more balanced and genuinely energised.

Our instructors hold highly recognised Pilates qualifications, including STOTT — a leading, anatomy-based certification that emphasises safe alignment, progressive programming and personalised modifications. Every session supports posture and joint health while building lasting strength.

FAQs

Find answers to your most common questions about our women-only Reformer Pilates classes.

What services do you offer?›

We offer a variety of Reformer Pilates classes: Foundations (beginner), Flow (intermediate), Strength (advanced), and Private sessions tailored to your body and goals.

How do I book?›

Booking is easy—simply select your class, date, and time to secure your spot. We recommend booking in advance to ensure availability for your preferred time slot.

What is your cancellation policy?›

We require a 24-hour notice for cancellations to avoid a cancellation fee. If you need to reschedule, please contact us as soon as possible.

What should I wear?›

Wear comfortable, flexible clothing. Grip socks are recommended to prevent slipping on the reformer. All classes are designed with focus on breath and precision.

Is Reformer Pilates suitable for beginners?›

Absolutely! Our Reformer Foundations class is designed specifically for beginners. The reformer provides support and resistance, making it accessible for all fitness levels. Our highly qualified instructors will guide you through proper form and progression.

Can I do Reformer Pilates if I have an injury?›

Yes. Reformer Pilates can be adapted for injury recovery and rehabilitation. Please mention any injuries during booking so we can modify movements and provide appropriate modifications. We recommend consulting your physiotherapist beforehand.

How long before I see results?›

Most clients notice improved posture, core strength, and muscle tone within 4–6 weeks of consistent practice (2–3 classes per week). Results depend on your starting fitness level, consistency, and individual goals.

Do I need prior Pilates experience?›

No prior experience necessary. Every class is designed for mixed abilities. Our instructors provide modifications for beginners and progressions for advanced practitioners, so everyone can work at their own level.

What's the difference between Drop-in and Class Packs?›

Drop-ins are one-off classes with no commitment. Class Packs offer better value and help maintain consistency—available in various sizes to suit your schedule. Check our Pricing page for current rates.

Can I freeze or transfer my class pack?›

Contact us directly at themethodroom@gmail.com or WhatsApp us to discuss your options. We're happy to work with you on flexibility where possible.

Do you offer private sessions?›

Yes! We offer Private Reformer sessions (1:1) and Duo Private sessions (2:1). Private sessions are fully customized to your body, goals, and fitness level. Email us at themethodroom@gmail.com or WhatsApp us to enquire, or check our Pricing page for rates.
