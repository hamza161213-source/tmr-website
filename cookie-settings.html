Cookie Settings

Last updated: December 19, 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide a better experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our site.

2. How We Use Cookies

The Method Room uses cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Improve website functionality and performance
  • Understand how visitors interact with our site
  • Enable booking system integration
  • Provide personalised content and recommendations

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies (Always Active)

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, booking system access, and form submissions. You cannot opt out of these cookies.

  • Session cookies: Maintain your session as you navigate the site
  • Security cookies: Authenticate users and prevent fraudulent activity
  • Form cookies: Remember information you enter in booking and contact forms

Performance and Analytics Cookies

These cookies collect information about how visitors use our website, such as which pages are visited most often. This helps us improve our site's performance and user experience.

  • Google Analytics: Tracks visitor behaviour and site usage
  • Website performance: Monitors load times and technical issues

Analytics Cookies: Allow us to understand how visitors use our site

Functional Cookies

These cookies allow the website to remember choices you make (such as your preferences) and provide enhanced, personalised features.

  • Preference cookies: Remember your settings and choices
  • Language preferences: Store your preferred language
  • Video playback: Enable embedded video content

Functional Cookies: Enable personalised features and preferences

Marketing and Advertising Cookies

These cookies track your online activity to help us deliver more relevant advertising and measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.

  • Social media cookies: Track interactions with our social media content
  • Advertising cookies: Display relevant ads based on your interests
  • Retargeting cookies: Show you relevant ads after visiting our site

Marketing Cookies: Deliver personalised advertising

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by third-party services that appear on our pages:

  • Google Maps: Powers our location map and directions
  • Mindbody: Enables our booking system integration
  • Social media platforms: Instagram, TikTok embeds and share buttons
  • Google Analytics: Website analytics and performance tracking

5. How Long Do Cookies Last?

  • Session cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies: Remain on your device for a set period (typically 30 days to 2 years) or until you delete them

6. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies. Check your browser's help menu for instructions. Note that blocking all cookies may impact website functionality.

  • Chrome
  • Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Opt-Out Tools

  • Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Your Online Choices (EU)
  • Network Advertising Initiative Opt-Out

7. Do Not Track Signals

Some browsers include a "Do Not Track" feature. Our website does not currently respond to Do Not Track signals, but you can manage cookies through the preferences above.

8. Changes to Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Settings page from time to time. Changes will be posted here with an updated "Last updated" date.
