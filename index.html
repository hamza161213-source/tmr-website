Absolutely! Our Reformer Foundations class is designed specifically for beginners. The reformer provides support and resistance, making it accessible for all fitness levels. Our STOTT-qualified instructors will guide you through proper form and progression.

Yes. Reformer Pilates can be adapted for injury recovery and rehabilitation. Please mention any injuries during booking so we can modify movements and provide appropriate modifications. We recommend consulting your physiotherapist beforehand.
